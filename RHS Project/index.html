Institutional Profile

Established as a premier boarding institution in the Kirehe District of Rwanda's Eastern Province, Rusumo High School stands as a beacon of academic rigor and character formation. Strategically positioned near the vibrant trans-African trade corridors, our campus offers a secure, focused, and state-of-the-art environment where students from across the nation converge to pursue excellence.

Student Life & Co-Curriculars

Athletics and Sports Disciplines

Physical fitness forms a key pillar of our daily routine. Rusumo High School actively competes in inter-school tournaments across the following sports:

  • Football: Senior boy's and girl's varsity teams with a regional pitch.
  • Basketball: Dedicated outdoor hardcourt hosting district matches.
  • Volleyball & Athletics: Focus areas for local talent development.

Student Clubs & Intellectual Societies

  • Anti-Crime & Discipline Club: Partnering with local authorities to promote drug abuse awareness.
  • Never Again & Peace Club: Fostering historical consciousness, unity, and reconciliation values.
  • Environmental Conservation Assembly: Spearheading internal campus waste mitigation.

Science Tournaments & Technical Achievements

Our students regularly participate in national innovation challenges. In recent science tournaments, Rusumo teams designed a low-cost solar-powered water filtration prototype explicitly tailored for rural farms across Kirehe.

Community Give-Back Initiatives

In harmony with local administrative mandates, our boarding community frequently executes community development projects. During structured outreach windows, students contribute to infrastructure repair and local environmental campaigns within neighboring villages.
